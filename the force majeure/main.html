Climate Resilience: The Map of The Future

Our project shines a spotlight on local organizations in San Diego that are championing climate resilience and driving positive change in their communities. Through partnerships, students from High Tech High Mesa collaborated with these organizations to support their missions. We've created a digital map and website that celebrates their impactful work, hoping to inspire people to take action and contribute to the remarkable efforts of these change-makers.
