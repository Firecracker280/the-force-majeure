Climate Resiliancy: The Map of The Future

Our project shines a spotlight on local organizations in San Diego that are championing climate resilience and driving positive change in their communities. Through partnerships, students from High Tech High Mesa collaborated with these organizations to support their missions, spanning from environmental sustainability to social justice. We've created a digital map and website that celebrates these organizations and their impactful work. Through this tool, we hope to inspire people to take action and volunteer and contribute to the remarkable efforts of these change-makers.
